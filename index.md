## What to Look for in a Drift Miata for Sale
Common Issues to Check

When inspecting a drift miata for sale, pay close attention to several common problem areas:

Rust: Check the rocker panels, frame rails, rear shock mounts, and engine bay around the battery area

Oil Leaks: Look for oil patches beneath the engine, often from old gaskets

Timing Belt: Ask when the timing belt was last replaced—slippage can cause power loss

Soft Top Condition: Cracks from sun exposure can lead to leaks

For a dedicated drift build, a clean shell with a healthy engine is worth more than a perfect interior that you'll likely strip anyway.

Suspension and Steering

A quality drift miata for sale find may already have some suspension work, but if not, coilovers are the first priority. Drift-spec units like the D2 Racing Drift Spec feature monotube inverted designs that handle the lateral forces and high temperatures of drifting.

Steering angle is equally critical. The IRP angle kit, designed for NA/NB Miatas, increases steering lock to over 65° and widens the front track by 100mm. This allows the deep, controlled slides that define competitive drifting.
